Critically, Gaia is not controlled by any single entity, not even Aetherian Dynamics. Its governance is managed by a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) with representatives from every nation that participates in the network. Decisions about Gaia's priorities are made through a consensus-based process, ensuring that the AI serves the collective good of the planet.
