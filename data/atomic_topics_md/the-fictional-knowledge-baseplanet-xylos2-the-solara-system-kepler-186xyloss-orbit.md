Xylos is an anomaly in the system. It is significantly larger than the other planets, with a radius about 2.5 times that of Earth. Its orbit is highly elliptical, taking it from the outer edges of the habitable zone to the frigid outer reaches of the star system over a period of 1,200 Earth days.
