In the months that followed, there was a mass exodus from the platforms and services of the Pillars. People and businesses flocked to new, decentralized alternatives—social networks built on peer-to-peer protocols, cloud storage solutions that used distributed file systems, and communication apps that were encrypted and controlled by the users, not a central company.
